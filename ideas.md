## Option A: Editorial Brutalism — "The Zine"

**Design Movement:** Post-digital editorial brutalism meets independent publishing zine culture

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw typographic hierarchy — text IS the design
2. Deliberate asymmetry and tension between elements
3. Monochrome base with a single jarring accent (electric lime or blood orange)
4. Visible grid lines as decorative structure

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: near-black (#0D0D0D) — commands authority
- Text: off-white (#F5F0E8) — warmth in the darkness
- Accent: electric lime (#C8FF00) — disruptive energy for CTAs and highlights
- Emotional intent: "This studio doesn't beg for attention. It earns it."

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed sections with hard-cut edges (no rounded corners)
- Oversized section numbers as background watermarks
- Text columns that break the grid intentionally
- Services listed as a "rate card" — newspaper classified style

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thick horizontal rules (4–8px) as section dividers
2. Oversized, stacked display type for hero (Clash Display or Space Grotesk)
3. "CREATOR SITE" badge in lime green pill — always visible in nav

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover states: text color inversion (white on lime)
- Scroll-triggered line reveals for section headings
- Cursor changes to crosshair on service cards

**Animation:**
- Hero text: staggered character-by-character fade-in
- Section transitions: horizontal slide-in from left
- CTA button: border-draw animation on hover

**Typography System:**
- Display: Clash Display Bold (oversized, 80–120px hero)
- Body: DM Mono (monospaced body text — editorial, raw)
- Labels: Space Grotesk Semibold (all-caps, tracked)

## Option B: Warm Modernism — "The Studio Wall"

**Design Movement:** Mid-century modern studio meets contemporary independent design practice

**Core Principles:**
1. Warm cream and terracotta palette — approachable expertise
2. Generous whitespace as a signal of confidence
3. Serif display type paired with geometric sans-serif body
4. Horizontal rhythm: everything breathes left-to-right

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: warm cream (#F7F3EC) — matches existing araw.studio aesthetic
- Text: deep charcoal (#1A1A18) — grounded, readable
- Accent: terracotta/amber (#C4622D) — warmth, craft, handmade quality
- Secondary: sage green (#7A9E7E) — for "available" / "quick turnaround" signals
- Emotional intent: "A craftsperson who takes their work seriously, but not themselves."

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Left-anchored layout with a persistent thin vertical rule
- Services displayed as a horizontal scrolling "menu board"
- Creator site service gets its own full-width "feature card" — visually distinct
- Pricing displayed as a clean comparison table, not hidden

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thin vertical rule running the full left edge of content
2. Hand-drawn underline SVG animations on key phrases
3. "7-day turnaround" badge as a stamp/seal graphic

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover: cards lift with subtle shadow (4px → 12px)
- Service cards expand to show more detail on hover
- Smooth scroll with section anchors

**Animation:**
- Hero: words fade in sequentially, italic word appears last with slight rotation
- Cards: stagger fade-up on scroll entry
- Pricing: numbers count up on scroll into view

**Typography System:**
- Display: Cormorant Garamond Italic + Bold (editorial, warm)
- Body: Instrument Sans (clean, contemporary)
- Labels: Instrument Sans Semibold, all-caps, 0.1em tracking

## Option C: Stark Precision — "The Rate Sheet"

**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets contemporary SaaS pricing clarity

**Core Principles:**
1. Information hierarchy is the aesthetic — nothing decorative that doesn't inform
2. Black and white with a single warm accent for CTAs
3. Grid is sacred — 12-column, everything snaps
4. Pricing is the hero — put the numbers front and center

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: pure white (#FFFFFF) — clinical clarity
- Text: pure black (#000000) — maximum contrast
- Accent: warm amber (#E8A020) — the only color allowed, used sparingly
- Emotional intent: "You know exactly what you're getting and what it costs."

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Newspaper-style multi-column layout for services section
- Pricing table as the dominant visual element above the fold
- Sticky header with service anchors
- Footer as a dense "contact card"

**Signature Elements:**
1. Oversized price tags as visual anchors
2. Thin rule separators (0.5px) between every content block
3. "AVAILABLE NOW" status indicator (blinking dot)

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- No decorative animations — only functional feedback
- Hover: background color inversion on service rows
- Focus: high-contrast outlines (accessibility-first)

**Animation:**
- Minimal: only fade-in on page load
- No scroll animations — content is immediately readable

**Typography System:**
- Display: Helvetica Neue Bold / Arial Black (pure Swiss)
- Body: Helvetica Neue Regular
- Mono: IBM Plex Mono for prices and technical details

## Selected Approach: **Option B — Warm Modernism "The Studio Wall"**

This best honors the existing araw.studio brand (warm cream palette, serif typography) while adding the clarity and directness needed to communicate the creator-focused 1-page website service. The terracotta accent adds warmth and craft, the left-anchored layout feels editorial and confident, and the "feature card" treatment for the creator site service makes it impossible to miss.
