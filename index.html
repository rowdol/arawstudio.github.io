What's breaking your product

01
No Design System
Without a shared system, every feature is rebuilt from scratch. Design and engineering fall out of sync. Inconsistency compounds silently until your product feels fractured — and fixing it costs 3× more than building it right the first time.
02
Accessibility Gaps
1 in 4 adults in the US has a disability. ADA lawsuits against digital products tripled in five years. If your product can't be navigated by keyboard or read by a screen reader, that exclusion is already costing you — in users, trust, and legal exposure.
03
No UX Direction
Building without a UX strategy means shipping based on assumptions. User needs go unvalidated, priorities drift, and the product diverges from the people it's meant to serve — often without anyone noticing until the metrics make it unavoidable.
